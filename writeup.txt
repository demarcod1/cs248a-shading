CS248A Shaders Project
Dominic DeMarco & Akram Sbaih
suids: demarcod, akram

Akram's implemented features:
  * Spotlights with smooth edges.
  * Shadows with smooth edges.

Dominic's implemented features:
  * Coordinate Transform
  * Phong Reflectance
  * Normal Mapping
  * Environment Lighting

New assets include:
  * Two new environment maps
    * suburban street
    * stadium
  * Three new materials and their associated normal maps
    * basketball
    * soccer ball
    * soocer field
  * Transformed variations on the original environment maps
    to enable a cool scene exploration effect.

All assets can be found in the media/textures folder.
